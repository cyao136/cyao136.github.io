About Me


I was born in the city of YueYang (岳阳), Hunan, China. At the age of 7, I left China for Canada, and learned my 2nd language: Français. Then, after another 7 years living in Montréal, I moved to Vancouver and learned English. I guess all that moving and learning new languages helped me with programming? :P

I had an obsession with technology. I would read tech news and breakthroughs all the time. It definitely helped to have experienced the birth/commercialization of LCDs, SSDs, smartphones... etc.

Needless to say, at the University of British Columbia, I went into Computer Science where I learned what coding and programming was. With 3 languages under my belt, a 4th one (Java) wasn't a challenge at all! And I loved it! It was everything I wanted: tech, challenging puzzles, new languages.

So, here I am, finishing up my degree in Cognitive Systems: Computational Intel&Design (a mouthful I know; put it simply: it's about AI and human-machine interaction), and looking forward to more awesome experience in the future!
